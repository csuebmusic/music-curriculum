Undergraduate Curriculum

B.A. in Music · complete document set, version 1.0

This is the curriculum documentation for the Theory and Musicianship sequence leading to the B.A. in Music. The collection includes two framework documents, which coordinate content, pacing, and benchmarks across the concurrent tracks, and twelve individual course syllabi covering MUS 108 through MUS 410.

The framework documents are intended for faculty and curriculum review. The syllabi are the student-facing versions distributed at the start of each semester.
