program modification: graduate sequence in music

prepared by i. thiebaut · spring 2026 · routing id cm-2026-014

01 rationale

This proposal consolidates two overlapping electives into a single course, revises the unit distribution across the graduate sequence, and aligns prerequisite language with the current catalog. The change reduces total required units by three while preserving every program learning outcome.

The revised sequence also clarifies the standing of one dual-listed (offered at two catalog levels) course, so that graduate and undergraduate enrollments are assessed against separate criteria. No new resources are required, and the affected courses already run on the existing rotation.
