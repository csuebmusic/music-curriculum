About These Templates

These are the syllabus templates for the Theory and Musicianship sequence. Each carries the shared section structure, deadline conventions, and house formatting, so an instructor copies the template for a given term and adapts dates, repertoire, and policies without rebuilding the frame. Pacing and end-of-semester benchmarks live in the Curriculum Master Plan; the section templates and deadline conventions they follow live in the Instructor's Companion.
